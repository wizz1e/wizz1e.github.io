DEERING - Robert A. Pragoff, 62, died suddenly Dec. 18, 2015, at Concord Hospital. Born in Manhattan, N.Y., he lived in the Bronx, N.Y., for most of his life, moving to Deering in 1983. He was the son of Harry and Cora Pragoff.Mr. Pragoff served as an instructor with the New Hampshire Department of Fire and Safety in Concord. From 1983 to his retirement in 2008 he was a member of the Deering Volunteer Fire Department. In his spare time, he was an avid photographer and traveller.He is survived by his wife, Margie Pragoff, of Deering; three children: Cindy Pragoff of Queens, N.Y., Robert Pragoff Jr. of Troy, N.Y., and Christopher Pragoff of Terceira Island, Azores, Portugal; two sisters: Catherine Pragoff of Goffstown and Barbara Wilson of Sugar Hill, Ga.; and several nieces and nephews.

SERVICES: Calling hours will be Monday, Dec. 21, from 6 to 9 p.m., at the New Hampshire Fire Academy, 98 Smokey Bear Blvd., Concord. Funeral services will be held Tuesday, at 10 a.m., also at the New Hampshire Fire Academy.In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Deering Volunteer Fire Department, 762 Deering Center Road, Deering, NH 03244. Following the service, lunch will be served at the Derryfield Restaurant, Mammoth Road, Manchester. All are invited to attend.For more information or to sign an online guest book, go to www.frenchandrising.com.
